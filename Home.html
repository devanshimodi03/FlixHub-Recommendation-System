FlixHub Recommendation System

A FlixHub recommendation system is a type of filtering system that predicts and suggests movies or films to users based on their preferences, past behavior, or other relevant data. These systems use algorithms to analyze large amounts of data, such as user ratings, genre preferences, and viewing history, to generate personalized movie recommendations. Recommendation systems are commonly used by streaming platforms, such as Netflix and Amazon Prime, to help users discover new content that aligns with their interests.
